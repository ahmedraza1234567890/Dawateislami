5 PILLARS OF ISLAM

Faith/Shahada

Beleif in the Unity of Allah & Prophet Muhammad ﷺ as Allah's messenger.

The act of worshipping Allah five times a day throughout your lifetime.

Alms/Zakat

Sharing a certain percentage of your wealth with less fortunate every year

Fasting/Roza

Fasting from dawn to dusk for 30 says in the holy month of Ramadan.

Pilgrimage/Hajj

A spriritual duty that every Muslim must offer once in their life, if able.
